## What's Inside

This guide walks you through the three things most buyers skip — and regret later. **Getting properly pre-approved** (not just pre-qualified) means sellers take you seriously from day one. **Setting up real-time MLS alerts** through your agent means you see new listings before they hit Zillow. And **knowing the NoCo market by city** means you're not wasting time touring homes in the wrong area.

The guide also includes a city comparison table covering Fort Collins, Loveland, Windsor, and Greeley — median prices, average days on market, and what each city is best for.

## Who This Is For

You're relocating to Northern Colorado — or seriously considering it — and you want to do this right. Maybe you've been casually browsing Zillow for a few weeks and you're realizing the market moves faster than you expected. This guide is for buyers who are 30-90 days out from being ready to make a move and want to get set up properly so they don't miss the right house when it comes along.

Michael Potter is a Northern Colorado real estate agent who specializes in helping out-of-state buyers relocate to NoCo. Every tip in this guide comes from working with buyers who made the move from California, Texas, and the Midwest.
